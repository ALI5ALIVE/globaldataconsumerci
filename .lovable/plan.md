### Key Principles Applied

1. **Definition-first**: The Intelligence Gap is defined in one sentence before explaining why it exists
2. **Sequence logic**: Problem → Definition → Causes → Impact → Bottom Line
3. **Enterprise language**: "Markets reward speed" not "Your consumers are changing"
4. **Three, not four**: Root causes simplified to 3 (Fragment, Debate, Late) for memorability
5. **Action-oriented**: "Operates, not just Reports" - focus on what the brand gets
6. **Clean metrics**: 70% faster, 2x success, 30% lower TCO - consistent across slides
