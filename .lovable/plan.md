

# Rewrite Consumer Journey Narration — World-Class Trusted Advisor, Under 5 Minutes

## Current State
- 12 narration entries (slideIds 0–11), many for slides that no longer exist (Platform at 8, Nothing Like This at 10)
- Total word count: ~1,800 words (~13 minutes at 140 wpm) — far too long
- Tone: informative but repetitive, lists stats multiple times, lacks natural energy and transitions

## Target
- 11 scripts matching the current deck (slideIds 0–10)
- Under 700 words total (~5 minutes at 140 wpm)
- Tone: trusted advisor with energy — confident, empathetic, not salesy
- Each slide script is a natural bridge to the next — no hard stops
- Best practice: open with tension, pivot with vision, prove with evidence, close with urgency

## Key Writing Principles
- Every script ends with a natural bridge phrase that makes the listener want the next slide
- No repeating the same stat across multiple slides (e.g., "60% reconciling" appears once)
- Second person throughout — "you", "your team"
- Short sentences. Spoken rhythm. Pauses built into punctuation
- Confident but never arrogant — "let me show you" not "we're the best"
